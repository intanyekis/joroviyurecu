Lacey Evans In-Ring Return, Asuka vs. Bianca Belair Set For 5/30 WWE RAW

Publish date: 2024-06-01

She returned to WWE on the April 8 episode of WWE SmackDown after she took over a year off due to her pregnancy, and has been featured in vignettes and promos since then. Her match next week will mark the first time she has competed since the February 15, 2021, episode of WWE RAW.

Evans has been away from WWE since last February, when she took a some time off because she was pregnant. She later welcomed her second daughter into the world in October. With Evans now seemingly back and in the mix of things, she’ll look to re-establish herself as one of the premier stars in the WWE women’s division.

Plus, less than a week before they collide at WWE Hell in a Cell, RAW Women’s Champion Bianca Belair will face Asuka in a non-title bout. On the May 16 episode, Asuka defeated Becky Lynch to earn a title shot. On Monday night, Lynch beat “The Empress of Tomorrow” in a rematch; as a result, she was added to the clash between Asuka and “The EST” at WWE Hell in a Cell. During the match, Asuka accidentally kicked Belair, so the champion will likely aim to get some revenge next week.
