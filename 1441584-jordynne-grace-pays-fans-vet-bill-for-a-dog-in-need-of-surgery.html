Jordynne Grace Pays Fans Vet Bill For A Dog In Need Of Surgery

Publish date: 2024-08-22

Jordynne Grace continues to do amazing things in and out of the ring in 2024.

TNA Knockouts Champion Jordynne Grace made a surprise appearance in the WWE Royal Rumble last month, but that isn’t all of the amazing things she’s done in the past week.

Indie artist and wrestling fan JRock ran into some unexpected medical expenses due to his dog Molly Moo needing surgery. Grace, being the dog lover that she is, reached out to JRock through Bleacher Report’s Chris Mueller to ask how much Molly Moo’s vet bills would be, tweeting out:

“DM me what the vet bill is,” Jordynne Grace said in a post.

JRock posted on his social media hours later confirming that Grace helped cover Molly Moo’s vet bill, tweeting out:

“Just want to give some appreciation to Jordynne for this. She literally helped save our Molly Moo, and it means the most! *Red heart emoji*,” JRock said in a post.

Grace will be in action next week on TNA iMPACT! teaming with Trinity (WWE’s Naomi) to take on Gisele Shaw and Savannah Evans.
