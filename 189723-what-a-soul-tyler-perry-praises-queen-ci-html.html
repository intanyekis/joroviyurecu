Tyler's post saw the actress seated in front of him, she donned a dark-colored dress and had a glammed up face, while he stood behind her in a wide-eyed grin.

Cicely Tyson and Tyler Perry at the Netflix Premiere of "A Fall From Grace" at Metrograph on January 13, 2020 in New York City.| Photo:Getty Images
