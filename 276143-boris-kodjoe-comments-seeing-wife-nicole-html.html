Boris Kodjoe Comments after Seeing Wife Nicole Ari Parker Showing Blonde Hair in Skimpy Top

Publish date: 2024-07-14

Boris Kodjoe is once again showing his fans how important it is to show love and support to their significant other. He recently dove into her comment section to show his affection.

Boris Kodjoe is all about keeping the flames alive! His relationship with Nicole Ari Parker is one of the long-lived ones in Hollywood, and he is obviously determined to keep things that way.

The handsome actor and his wife have in the past emphasized how important it is to keep the magic in a relationship going by doing things a couple used to do before they tie the knot. So it did not surprise many when he was observed expressing his mind in her comment section.

Boris Kodjoe as Andy on the third season of the show "Station 19" | Photo: Getty Images

The talented woman had shared a throwback picture of herself that captured her in a car wearing a skimpy top that showed some cleavage. She wore ripped jeans and had her hair dyed blonde.

It was a great look which is why Nicole expressed a desire to recreate the hair color in her caption. Shortly after the post went live, Boris joined many who flocked to the comment section to express their opinions.

While many went on and on about how gorgeous she looked, Boris opted to use flame emojis to express himself, most likely a subtle way of giving his approval for her proposed hairstyle.

If nothing else, it proved exactly how dedicated and aware they are of each other, which in turn explains how the two have kept their relationship afloat for nearly two decades. Here are a few important things to note about the couple.

THEY MADE OUT WHEN THEY FIRST MET

Boris and Nicole had been working together on "Soul Food" for about two years before their relationship took off. In fact, the first time they met, he made out with her in front of the cameras.

They spent the day frolicking on the streets, having fun, just like teenagers would.

Their first date was in the year 2002, and it did not go down at a fancy restaurant; instead, they attended the Arthur Ashe Foundation Gala in New York City. They went on to get married in 2005 and now have two children they dote on.

THAT YEAR NICOLE REVEALED TOO MUCH

Nicole and Boris's relationship is one of the hottest and long-lived in the entertainment world; however, that does not exempt their paradise from experiencing occasional storms now and then.

An example of such a storm was the one they encountered in 2020. At the time, Nicole started a controversy when she implied that the embers of their flame might be burning low.

Apparently, she felt he was not doing enough and had begun to get so comfortable; he stopped doing things that made her feel like his girlfriend.

This made her feel disconnected from him, and she was very candid about it in a video that began to circulate as the controversy gained steam.

She said, "I don't necessarily want to be single, but I miss the single life. I miss the text messages for no reason. I miss the coming up behind me when I'm scrambling eggs and just kissing me on the back of the neck."

Boris Kodjoe's comment on his wife, Nicole Ari Parker's Instagram post | Photo: Instagram/ nicoleariparker

Boris did not look too happy about what his wife was saying in the video, but he held his tongue until netizens started to link Nicole's statement to possible infidelity. Then, he clamped down on the narrative with a long message he shared via Instagram.

The statement discredited talks of a breakup, and above all, it showed that Boris had listened to his wife complain and had chosen to work on his shortcomings because he ended the writeup by declaring that he would be "kissing the back of her neck for no reason."

That seems to be working out well for the couple because things seem to be back to normal between them. Boris has been doing more, and when they celebrated their anniversary this year, it was clear that the two were still very much in love with each other.

They spent the day frolicking on the streets, having fun, just like teenagers would. Fans hope their love works out in the end.
