WWE Live Event Results (7/11): Nashville, TN Rollins/Ambrose, Reigns/Wyatt

Publish date: 2024-05-25

(1) Jack Swagger def. Fandango. What’s going on with Swagger? He’s been working live shows for months now and has no place on television? 

(2) Curtis Axel & Damien Sandow def. The Miz & Adam Rose. This was basically a comedy tag team match. The booking doesn’t make sense for Miz; isn’t he in a title match soon with two people on the other WWE tour? 

(3) Cesaro def. Kane. Good match. 

(4) The Lucha Dragons def. The Ascension. 

(5) Randy Orton def. Sheamus. Solid match. Orton was obviously very, very over with the house, and Sheamus got a ton of heat and the usual chants. 

(6) Natalya won a Divas battle royal. Most of the Divas who weren’t on the other tour were here. Emma, Cameron, Summer, Foxy, the rare appearance from Layla. A couple others I think, but I can’t remember. 

(7) Roman Reigns def. Bray Wyatt. 

(8) Seth Rollins def. Dean Ambrose to retain the WWE world title in a Street Fight. Wyatt came out and hit Ambrose with Sister Abigail which led to the end of the match. Reigns rushed the ring and made the save, and the two babyfaces cleaned house and hit their finishers to send the fans home happy.
