Britney Is Hanging With An Ex-Employee Charged With A Felony

According to Page Six, Britney has been getting close to Paul Richard Soliz, who was hired a year ago to work as the singer’s housekeeper, though he’s no longer her employee. “I’m concerned,” the source told the outlet.

For now, it’s unclear if the nature of Soliz and Britney’s relationship is romantic or not.
