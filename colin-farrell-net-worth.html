Colin James Farrell has an estimated net worth of $30 million.  Irish golden globe-winning actor, Farrell kick started his career playing small roles in British dramas.

His breakthrough came in 1998, when he featured in the Hollywood movie "The War Zone". Since then, there has been no turning back for this talented actor. He has appeared in numerous movies including "Miami vice", "Tigerland", "Phone booth", "Daredevil" and "SWAT".

Farrell's most prominent role came when he played a starring role in the movie "Tigerland". Colin Farrell's strongest point is his ability to naturally use his Irish accent in American movies. This has had a captivating effect on his audience. In fact, in the year 2003, people magazine voted Farrell among the 50 most beautiful people in the world. This was followed by a sixth place listing by company magazine, on the sexiest men in the world.
