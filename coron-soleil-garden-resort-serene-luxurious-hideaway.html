Coron Soleil Garden Resort - Serene &amp; Luxurious Hideaway The Talks Today

Publish date: 2024-06-24

Here’s why Coron Soleil Garden Resort is one of the best accommodations in Coron, Palawan.

CORON SOLEIL GARDEN RESORT – The perfect place for a perfect Palawan vacation is this resort for premium services and facilities.

One of the most famous Palawan destinations is Coron which is considered one of the top tourist destinations in the country frequented by both locals and foreigners. This tropical island destination is known for its world-class World War II-era wreck diving, limestone karst landscapes, freshwater lakes, and shallow-water coral reefs.

And if you are heading here, one of the best places where you can spend your vacation days is the Soleil Garden Resort.

This is located on Mabentangen Road and is near in many popular attractions in and around Coron. Guests can request pick-up and drop-off at the airport but for an additional fee.

As stated on their website, this premium resort offers “premium amenities and facilities for the luxurious, indulgent and leisure traveler who yearns for relaxing and worry-free travel”.

In terms of accommodation, they have a handful of room types such as Deluxe Queen, Deluxe Twin, Poolside Premiere, and Poolside Suite.

DINING

  • They have a restaurant that serves international, Filipino, and Coron dishes. 
  • Their pool bar is located in a convenient location where guests can enjoy a relaxing time while slipping in their favorite drink.
  • The Skybar is on the roof deck of the main building which can accommodate up to 30 people.

The resort also has function rooms where events and other social affairs can be down.
