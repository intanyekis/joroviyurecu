Days of Our Lives Spoilers April 22, 2024 Through May 3, 2024

Publish date: 2024-06-26

We have the latest Days of Our Lives spoilers from Monday, April 22, to Friday, May 3. Konstantin hopes that someone might help him out of a potential situation, while John finds himself in a familiar position thanks to a bit of mind control. Meanwhile, as Sloan tells Leo the last thing he wants to hear, Eric finally comes right out and asks his wife the billion-dollar question! Read on for all the details….

Days of Our Lives spoilers week of April 22:

Days of Our Lives spoilers for Monday, April 22:

In today’s recap, Stefan blocks Ava from finding Clyde’s book, and then faces Harris’ wrath.

Paulina didn’t score any points with Abe and Kayla by rushing off to try and help Chanel! Meanwhile, given that the honeymoon was a complete and total bust (to say the least), Johnny decides the least he can do is surprise his new wife!

With Clyde breathing down their neck, Harris and Ava attempt to reclaim the crime boss’ little black book from the Bistro.

Kate thought by now, her son’s name has been cleared. Will venting to Roman about the situation help?

Days of Our Lives spoilers for Tuesday, April 23:

In today’s recap, Eric connects Sloan to the Salem Inn, Theresa’s news infuriates Alex, and Nicole ponders her future.

Sloan had to know this was coming: Eric calls her out on the fact that their budget is definitely out of whack. She’ll have to think fast in order to cover her tracks!

Cue the bow-chicka-bow-bow music, because Theresa is going to steam things up by giving Alex a massage!

In case there are any questions whatsoever about the future of DiMera Enterprises, EJ fills Kristen in on his plans for the family business.

Brady and Nicole manage to share a nice moment reflecting on their friendship.

Fans can wonder no more: Kristian Alfonso is bringing Hope home.

Days of Our Lives spoilers for Wednesday, April 24:

In today’s recap, Everett, or should we say Bobby’s, behavior sets Stephanie on edge, and Kristen targets Wendy while investigating Li’s murder.

Kristen and Stefan ponder who might have killed Li, running through a list of suspects which will likely include one another. (As for us, we’re willing to bet he’s not really dead. After all, Salem’s not the kind of town where death is permanent!)

When Tripp plans a surprise for Wendy, will they wind up on the same page?

Their last effort at finding the little black book having proven fruitless, Ava and Harris give it a go once again!

Rafe has no time to play good cop, so he slips into bad cop mode as he issues a warning to Stefan.

Days of Our Lives spoilers for Thursday, April 25:

In today’s recap, Alex and Kristen hit the sheets, and Maggie asks Konstantin to sign a prenup.

Sarah and Xander have been through enough that nothing should shock them. But Maggie proves that’s not true by revealing her intention to marry Konstantin! Meanwhile, the groom-to-be is doing everything he can to drive a wedge between longtime pals John and Steve. (Good luck with that, dude!)

Kristen suggests that she and Alex spend a little time together. As in… the night!

When Theresa says she feels like a big ol’ failure, will Brady convince her otherwise?

Days of Our Lives spoilers for Friday, April 26:

In today’s recap, Sloan panics upon hearing Dimitri is being released from prison, Eric doesn’t trust the answers he receives, and Chanel’s not feeling herself.

Determined to find answers to his and Sloan’s financial problems, Eric decides to follow the money… only to have it lead him to the Salem Inn and Leo!

Nicole hopes that Chad might consider hiring her to work at The Spectator.

Paulina reads EJ up one side and down the other!

Johnny and Chanel are looking for a new place to live. (This being a soap, what do you wanna bet they wind up moving into a set we’re already familiar with?

Days of Our Lives spoilers week of April 29:

Days of Our Lives spoilers for Monday, April 29:

Maggie has proven smarter than Konstantin probably thought she’d be. But can Theresa help get him out of the pre-nup Maggie wants him to sign?

Days of Our Lives spoilers for Tuesday, April 30:

It wouldn’t be Salem if someone wasn’t having their brain taken over by someone else! Fully under Konstantin’s control, will John carry out the orders he’s being given?

Days of Our Lives spoilers for Wednesday, May 1:

Like many couples in this country, Eric and Sloan find themselves fighting over money. Of course, most people out there probably aren’t using the rent money to pay off blackmailers…

Days of Our Lives spoilers for Thursday, May 2:

Things go from awkward to comfortable between Eric and Nicole… maybe a little too comfortable?

Days of Our Lives spoilers for Friday, May 3:

Eric decides it’s time to get to the heart of the matter But how will Sloan respond when asked, point blank, if Leo is blackmailing her? Meanwhile, Leo fills EJ in on a secret that definitely falls under the category of “life-changing”!
