Draper James' New Version of Best-Selling Totes Y'all Bag

Publish date: 2024-08-12

https://www.instagram.com/p/2ZKVFbgIk0/

Totes on their game! After Draper James’ wildly popular Totes Y’all tote sold out twice, Reese Witherspoon‘s brand unveiled a brand-new take on the beloved bag, and Us Weekly has your first look!

PHOTOS: Reese’s Cool Mom Street Style

While the original version is an ivory canvas tote with navy straps, the new Totes Y’all bag will come in blue denim trimmed in white. Nevertheless, the jean edition will still boast the same Totes Y’all logo in a sweet script and gold hardware.

The sassy tote launched with the label in May 2015 and remains its best-selling piece to date. When Witherspoon stepped out rocking the piece, it sold out immediately. Since then, Draper James, which also offers Southern-inspired dresses, separates and accessories, has replenished their stock three more times.
