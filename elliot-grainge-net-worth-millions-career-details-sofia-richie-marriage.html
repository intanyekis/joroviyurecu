Sofia Richie married Elliot Grainge in a glamorous wedding in the South of France in April.

Grainge comes from a wealthy family. His dad is Universal Music Group chairman-CEO Lucian Grainge, one of the most powerful men in the music industry. The British businessman has held the position since 2011 and has worked with major artists like Lady Gaga, Amy Winehouse, Katy Perry, The Rolling Stones, and Rihanna.

Lucian was named Billboard's first-ever Executive of the Decade and received a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame. He was also knighted by Queen Elizabeth II in 2016 and is one of the most respect named in the business.

Instead of taking a role with his successful father, Elliot started his own record label. He did learn from one of the best in the musical industry. "I was a weird kid who used to read the music trades," he told Variety in a 2019 profile. "What I noticed about my father's negotiating style was how transparent, honest and sensible he was. When you put your cards on the table like that, negotiating is less competitive and more about teamwork."

How Sofia Richie And Elliot Grainge Met

Although the news of their 2022 engagement came as a shock to many, Sofia Richie and Elliot Grainge had known each other for years.

Sofia's father, the iconic singer Lionel Richie, was a frequent collaborator and friend of Grainge's father, Sir Lucian Grainge. The couple kept their relationship secret until April 2021, when they went official on Instagram.

"I love Elliot," Lionel Richie told Access Hollywood, shortly after his daughter's announcement. "I've known him since he was 12 … It was one of those things where I don't have to go back and check out the kid. I know who it is."

How Elliot Grainge Earns His Net Worth

After graduating from college, Elliot Grainge moved to LA and started the 10K Projects record label. Their roster includes artists such as Aitch, Between Friends, Damien Styles, Peach Tree Rascals, Taz Taylor, Lil Boom, and WizTheMC. The label has earned 18 gold and eight platinum/multi-platinum singles, with Grainge earning a spot on Forbes' 30 under 30 list in 2020.

According to the 10K Projects website, their purpose "is to recognize and champion the unique visions of the storytellers and creatives of tomorrow - as they see it, without compromise or regard for convention."
