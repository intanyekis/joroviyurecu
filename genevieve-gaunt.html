    Genevieve Gaunt Facts

  • She grew up in London, England, United Kingdom.
  • Genevieve Gaunt was around 12 years old when she started acting.
  • She made her first theatrical film appearance by playing the role of Pansy Parkinson, a Slytherin student who is described as pug-faced, in the 2004 fantasy film, Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban, starring Daniel Radcliffe, Rupert Grint, Emma Watson, Gary Oldman, Alan Rickman, Fiona Shaw, Emma Thompson, and Julie Walters.
  • From 2015 to 2018, she appeared as Wilhelmina “Willow” Moreno Henstridge, Liam’s friend who is later hired by Helena to be the family’s social media manager, in the American primetime television soap opera, The Royals. Genevieve Gaunt co-starred on the show alongside Elizabeth Hurley, William Moseley, Alexandra Park, Tom Austen, Merritt Patterson, and others.
  • She played the character named Issie in the 2016 British coming of age drama, Kids in Love, whose lead cast included Will Poulter, Alma Jodorowsky, Jamie Blackley, Cara Delevingne, and Gala Gordon.
  • In 2019, she brought to life the character of Princess Isabella, Queen Joan and King Philip’s daughter who is a beautiful, energetic, and self-aware woman, in the historical fiction drama television series, Knightfall.
  • As a theatre artist, Genevieve Gaunt has been cast as Diana Lake in French Without Tears by Terence Rattigan and as Amanda in Monogamy by Torben Betts.
