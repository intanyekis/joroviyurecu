Kaitlynn Carter is an American social media star, TV personality, and blogger, best known for her highly publicized short-term relationship with Miley Cyrus between August and September 2019. The two were friends for around 3 years. She stated: “It had never crossed my mind that I was even capable of loving a woman the way I loved her.” Kaitlynn has also amassed a large fanbase on her lifestyle blog, Hey, Miss Carter, also known as Western Wild, before she closed it down.
