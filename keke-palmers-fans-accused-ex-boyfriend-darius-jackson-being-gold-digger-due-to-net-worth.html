Recently, Keke Palmer has been at the center of many conversations. This is due to controversy surrounding her failed relationship with her baby's father, Darius Jackson.

For many people, seeing Keke Palmer at the center of such a controversy is disheartening, especially since some of Palmer's fans side-eyed Darius Jackson ever since he walked into the former Nickelodeon star's life. The questions about Darius Jackson's intentions naturally arose after fans did a little digging and found out that Darius Jackson isn't at Keke Palmer's level in terms of fortune and status.

Jackson didn't help his case when winning over Palmer's fans after he publicly humiliated her by calling her out on X (formerly known as Twitter) when she appeared alongside Usher at a concert in a subjectively revealing outfit. Now that the couple are officially done, fans who accused Darius Jackson of being a gold digger believe their argument is now indisputable. This article will cover that, as well as Palmer's dating rules within the industry and the accusations she leveled against Jackson.

Keke Palmer's Fans Believe She Dated A Gold Digger

After Darius Jackson's infamous tweet that called out his then-girlfriend and baby mama, fans of Keke Palmer flocked to X to put him in his place.

One X user tweeted, "Keke Palmer is basically the breadwinner in the relationship, and they aren't married yet...".

Another user echoed this sentiment, tweeting:

"Isn't Keke Palmer the bread winner... what is he talking about."

While doing so, the initial weariness and concern from Keke Palmer's fans resurfaced. This concern was that Darius Jackson was seeking clout and only dating Palmer for her growing fortune. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Keke Palmer's net worth is $7.5 million. This doesn't come as a shock due to her diligent and iconic work in the entertainment industry.

Keke Palmer Doesn't Date Men In The Entertainment Industry

Contrary to what fans may want for Keke Palmer, she prefers not to date within the entertainment industry, which is why, when she soft-launched her relationship with Darius Jackson, no one knew who he was. Even though she dipped her toes in the celebrity pool of men, her dating history within the entertainment industry is fairly short.

In a podcast episode of Baby, This Is Keke Palmer, Palmer revealed that she prefers to date outside the entertainment industry.

"Here's the thing about me: I am not that attracted to people in the industry at that level... I just want my whole life to be predicated on the industry."

She then made it clear that she wasn't trying to throw shade at men in the entertainment industry, saying that there were, in fact, plenty of nice people. However, Palmer's desire for a sliver of normalcy in her already public life turns her off from men in her field.

After that, Keke Palmer went on to reveal a misconception people have of celebrities dating other celebrities. "I've met nice guys in the industry... but I've always been like 'are you using me for clout?' See people assume celebrities should date together, you know... they feel like other people are looking for clout but celebrities [are] looking for clout more than the average person."

Keke Palmer Threw Shade At Darius Jackson Amid Abuse Allegations

Sadly, Keke Palmer's relationship ended in an unfortunate way. Months after announcing her pregnancy, the media caught wind of Palmer's filed restraining order against Darius Jackson. According to The Cut, the public court document listed physical and emotional abuse throughout their two-year relationship. Along with this, Palmer is seeking custody over their shared child, Leodis "Leo" Andrellton.

As details continue to unfold surrounding Keke Palmer and Darius Jackson's crumbling relationship, Keke Palmer didn't miss a beat when throwing overt shade at Darius Jackson in recent music videos.

One video came after the Usher debacle. The song is entitled "Boyfriend," which featured Keke Palmer alongside Usher. At the end, Keke Palmer answers a phone call reminding her of a show she apparently slept through. After realizing this, she comically said, "I'm so tired. I'm a mother after all." A clear diss at Darius Jackson's July 2023 tweet (which is now deleted) that said "It's the outfit tho... you a mom."

Then, as Keke Palmer fought for a restraining order and custody of her son, she released "Assets." The song quite literally embodied the attitude many of Palmer's fans had toward the discrepancy in pay between Palmer and Jackson. If the title and lyrics (and timing) of the song and video didn't clue you in on Palmer's shade, then the skits that appeared at the beginning and end of the video left no room for interpretation.

"These guys, man, they're a trip. I feel like they don't know what to do with a woman." Palmer said to a fictional Miss Pam (who is also played by a disguised Keke Palmer). And then later, as Palmer left the salon, Miss Pam shared her thoughts: "She always had it, didn't she? Baby daddy situation ghetto though. Gotta get that together."
