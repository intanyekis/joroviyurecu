Kendra Wilkinsons Dating Status Following Her Divorce from Her Ex Husband

Publish date: 2024-06-08

In October 2018, Kendra Wilkinson informed her followers on Twitter that she had signed her "last divorce paper."

The update came after she filed for divorce from her husband Hank Baskett in April that year.

Since the divorce, Wilkinson has been an open book about her dating life. But does she have a boyfriend at present?

Kendra Wilkinson Is Looking for Love after Divorce

The Girls Next Door star is not in a committed relationship at present. However, she has been casually dating men in search of a perfect partner.

Speaking with People ahead of the release of her new series Kendra Sells Hollywood in November 2021, she stated she was open to the idea of dating.

"Until now, I never really felt ready," Wilkinson shared. "But now, I want to explore that other side of me, and I hope to find love again. I want to fill my home with someone else's energy. It will be nice to have that again."

Kendra Wilkinson Dating History after Divorce

With an intention to find love again, the reality TV star first dated former Bachelorette contestant Chad Johnson after her divorce was finalized.

She was linked to Johnson in early 2019 after the two were spotted getting cozy while out to dinner in Los Angeles.

"For the millionth time. I'm not dating right now," she, however, tweeted in January that year. "I'm basically a [expletive] virgin."

At the same time, her best friend, Jessica Hall, also spoke with Us Weekly and expressed that she wanted to see her friend with a family man.

She stated that while revealing even Wilkinson was looking for a similar spouse.

Hall added, "She doesn't want the spotlight or the crazy success. She wants a guy that just wants to go camping and wants to take her and barbecue."

Following the Johnson chapter, she was linked to Donald "DJ" Friese and then with a mystery man. Speaking of the latter man, the California native was spotted with him in Los Angeles in April 2020.

Kendra Wilkinson Focused on Raising Kids

Apart from dating, Wilkinson is keeping her focus on providing a good upbringing to her two kids, whom she shares with her former husband, Baskett.

The former couple's first child, son Hank Baskett IV, was born in 2009, and the second child, daughter Alijah Mary Baskett, was born in 2014.

Kendra Wilkinson clicked with her ex-husband and two kids before the divorce. (Photo: Twitter)

In the previous conversation with People, she even expressed that she was a single mom and was looking to raise her kids well — while trying to explore herself, too.

As per her, she was still on talking terms with her ex, as both were committed to co-parenting the kids.

Moreover, Wilkinson and Baskett were very young when they had their wedding. The two reportedly tied the knot in 2009.

But while being together as a married couple, the two often faced various problems, from his lack of longevity in his career to his Super Bowl fiasco to her postpartum depression to his infidelity to money and career problems.
