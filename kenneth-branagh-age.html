Kenneth Branagh Wiki

Kenneth Branagh was born on December 10th 1960, he is an Irish-born English actor, director, and writer who is best known for his film adaptations of Shakespearean plays. He began acting in school plays and graduated from the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art in 1981.

Kenneth Branagh Wiki - Biography

Oscar-nominated director and actor Kenneth Branagh have recently made waves with his semi-autobiographical movie Belfast, in 2012 he was appointed a Knight Bachelor by Queen Elizabeth II. He has also won an Academy Award, four BAFTAs, two Emmy Awards, and a Golden Globe Award.

Kenneth Branagh Early Life & Career

Early Life - Kenneth Branagh was born in Belfast to Frances Branagh her mother and William Branagh his father who was a plumber as well as a joiner for fitting compartments, etc.

Kenneth Branagh was the middle one among his three siblings and used to live in the Tiger’s Bay area of the city.

His family moved to England when he was 9 years old, he got educated at Whiteknights Primary School and Meadway School and started training at the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art, London.

Career - Kenneth Branagh achieved early success in theatre in his native Northern Ireland for his role as Billy, the title character in the BBC's Play for Today. He won the SWET award for the best newcomer in 1982.

Kenneth Branagh's film career started with Henry V, Much Ado About Nothing, and Hamlet, he made his debut as a film director in Henry V. Twenty years later, he became the director for Marvel’s Thor, Disney’s Cinderella, and other blockbusters films.
