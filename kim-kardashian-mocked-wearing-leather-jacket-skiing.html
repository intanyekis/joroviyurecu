Kim Kardashian Mocked For Wearing $17,000 Leather Jacket While Skiing

Highlights

  • Kim Kardashian rocked a $17,000 leather jacket while skiing, showing off her posh style even in the snow.

  • Despite the high price tag, Kim didn't seem concerned about getting the expensive outfit dirty, embracing the snow during ski lessons with her family.

The Kardashians have put a lot of expensive things on their bodies, but when walking the red carpet or posing for an Instagram photo, there much opportunity for the clothing piece or accessory to get ruined. But it appears Kim wasn’t thinking about that when she rocked an $17,000 leather jacket during a skiing trip recently.

There’s no denying Kim looked posh in her all-black outfit, as paparazzi caught the reality star hitting the slopes. The star of her outfit was a Limited Edition Hooded Leather Sport Biker Jacket ($16,920), which cut off at her hips.

Kim paired the stunning jacket with a pair of matching ski pants with suspenders ($2,780). She finished the look with a simple black turtleneck underneath plus a matching scarf and dark ski goggles, which pushed her long hair back.

While Kim’s outfit was stylish, it was also ridiculously expensive. Everything together likely cost over $20,000.
