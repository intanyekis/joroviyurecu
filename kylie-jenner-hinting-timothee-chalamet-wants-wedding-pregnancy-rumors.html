Fans Think Kylie Was Hinting At A Wedding At The Met

Kylie wore a champagne-colored Oscar De La Renta dress for the occasion, which got flack for not seeming to adhere to the “Garden of Time” theme, or at least left fans scratching their heads as to how it matched.

But some fans thought Kylie’s look was more directed to Timothee than the gala itself, with one Reddit thread saying it looked bridal. "Maybe she’s hinting that she wanna get wifed up soon," one person wrote.

"This looks like off the rails cheap bride gown," a different comment said, while another added, "This is giving 'I may be 45 but I'm still gonna wear white to my 3rd marriage' energy.”

Other speculated it may be a reference to Kylie’s long-time desire to be a bride, which didn’t happen in her relationship with Travis Scott, even though they share two kids. "I feel like this is the wedding dress she didn’t get to use. Which now makes me think her Met looks are the dresses she would’ve married her exes in had they not bounced," one person said.

"I feel like she’s always wearing wedding dresses," another added, s which seemed to reference Kylie’s 2022 look, a bridal inspired gown designed by the late Virgil Abloh. Kylie said the idea was to be “married” to the met in the full dress.

It’s Unclear If Kylie And Timothee Are Still Together

Even though there have been allegations Kylie is pregnant, there’s also speculation over whether she and Timothee are still together. The couple haven’t been spotted together in months, with some reports claiming they’re on a break due to conflicting schedules.

"They’re hardly together anymore,” a source told OK! Magazine. “Their lives got busy, and they drifted apart. Of course, once their schedules quiet down, that could all change."

Apart from her recent appearance in New York to attend the Met, Kylie has largely been in Los Angeles, dedicating her time to promotional activities for her latest venture, Sprinter vodka seltzer. Conversely, Timothee has been traveling between locations while filming of the upcoming Bob Dylan biopic.
