Lil Uzi Vert Gifts JT New Maserati For 31st Birthday

Publish date: 2024-07-15

JT has a brand new ride and Lil Uzi is to thank. The City Girls rapper celebrated her 31st birthday on Dec. 3, 2023, and the lavish gifting included luxury handbags and a vehicle.

In clips shared across social media, the Miami native showed appreciation for her gold and white Chanel purse, writing, “love it, thank you baby.” Her “peaceful pretty birthday” was complete with pink and gold decorations including balloons and fresh flowers.

An additional video showcases the couple in a Maserati, also gifted to JT by Lil Uzi Vert. According to HipHopDX, the car is an MC20 model from F.C. Kerbeck — the same dealership mentioned by the Philadelphia native on the leaked track “Pardon Me.”

For the “No Bars” performer’s 30th birthday, her “Just Wanna Rock” beau executed a candlelit dinner, with gifts from Eliantte and a brand new Rolls Royce Phantom.

The two rappers have been an item since 2021 and their relationship has been marked by public displays of affection, expensive gifts, rap lyrics, and the occasional spat. Earlier this year, the couple was involved in an altercation during the BET Awards, which they have seemingly resolved.
