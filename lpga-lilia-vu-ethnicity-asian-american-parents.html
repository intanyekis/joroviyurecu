Lilia Vu Ethnicity – Is She Asian American?

Lilia is of Vietnamese descent, and she holds American nationality.

As an Asian American, Lilia is very proud of her heritage and her grandfather’s sacrifice to immigrate to the United States after the Vietnam War ended in 1932. 

According to Lilia, Her grandfather went out for months without telling anyone in the family where he went to. Later it was found that he was building a boat alone to escape the whole village from Vietnam. 

Because the boat was filled with many people, it started to sink. But her grandfather didn’t lose hope; he just looked at the sky and hoped someone would help them.

Luckily, An American ship came by and helped all the people and moved to America.

That’s how Lilia’s grandfather saved his family and other villagers from the war and migrated to the USA.

Due to his great sacrifice and courage, she is always motivated and inspired by her grandfather. 

Lilia Vu Parents And Ethnicity

Lilia Vu is the daughter of a Vietnamese couple, Kieu Thuy and Douglas Vu. She shares the same parents as her brother, Andre Vu, who is also into golfing. 

Her brother, Andre Vu, has played on the UC Riverside golf team and is following in his sister’s footsteps.

Lilia is known for being very close with her family, and she views her parents and grandfather as the most significant role models and inspiration.

As it is pretty clear that she came from Vietnam, she is an Asian. Besides that, there hasn’t been much discussion regarding her ethnicity.
