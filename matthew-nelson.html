Who was Matthew Nelson? Learn more about him

Matthew Nelson was an American television writer and producer. He served as a co-executive producer and story editor for both “Boy Meets World” (BMW) and “Girl Meets World” (GMW). His writing skills were recognized when his script for “Girl Meets 1961” was nominated for a Writers’ Guild award, while “Girl Meets the Forgiveness Project” was up for a Humanities Prize. He was an integral voice in the BMW and GMW communities, and his loss has affected many in the industry.

How did Matthew Nelson die? What was his cause of death?

At the time of this publication, there was no cause of death reported for Matthew Nelson.
