Publish date: 2024-06-12

Nicola Peltz is an American actress and model who is known for her roles in films like The Last Airbender and Transformers: Age of Extinction. Peltz is also known as the youngest child of billionaire Nelson Peltz.

And, as an actress and the future heiress of her father’s billion dollars worth corporation, Peltz is extremely private about her personal life. But, she isn’t so private about her love life, though. Nicola is currently dating Brooklyn Beckham and insights into their love life are common sights on the actress Instagram feed.

Nicola Peltz & Brooklyn Beckham Are In Love Love

Rumors of Nicola Peltz and budding photographer Brooklyn Beckham being romantically linked sparked in late 2019. But, Nicola and the son of David Beckham only confirmed their romance only a year later in 2020.

It was Brooklyn to confirm the relationship by sharing a bathroom selfie of the young love birds on his Instagram. And, since then the couple has been constantly sharing numerous pictures of the two. Some of the lovey-dovey snaps also showcase PDA.

Both parties strongly believe that their love story will make it to the end. In May 2020, Nicola posted a series of pictures where they can be seen holding hands and the Deck the Halls captioned the picture with, ‘i love you’.
