Peter Rosenberg's Bio

Peter Rosenberg was born on 23 July 1979 in Chevy Chase, Maryland.

He grew up alongside his siblings in Maryland where he attended Bethesda-Chevy Chase High School before moving to the University of Maryland for a degree.

His Brother, Nick has always impacted him since he was a young kid. His older brother, who had an enormous love for hip-hop, introduced him to this music genre.
