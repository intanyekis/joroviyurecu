Pro Wrestling is a highly profitable sports industry, and the richest pro wrestlers of 2021 are making millions. While most people are well aware that wrestling is a rare mix of sports and entertainment, it is very thrilling and has hundreds of thousands of fans worldwide.

The following is a list of the ten richest pro wrestlers of 2021.

Bella Twins

The Bella Twins, Nikki and Brie, are pro wrestlers and famous American models. They have been seen in various movies and TV programs.

The twins have held the precursor to the modern-day WWE Women's Title, the butterly winged Diva's championship. They both were also still associated with WWE after they retired. This year, they were inducted into the prestigious WWE Hall of Fame. Collectively, the Bella sisters have a net worth of an astounding $20 million.

Stacy Keibler

Stacy Keibler is another American wrestler who has had a versatile career in modeling and acting. She was also once a cheerleader and a dancer.

Currently, she is a retired pro wrestler. However, she has built a fancy net worth of about $25 million during her days of wrestling. She is renowned for her career with WWE and World Championship Wrestling.

Kurt Angle

Kurt Angle is known for leaving WWE to join Impact Wrestling in a highly publicized way, although eventually, he found his way back to World Wrestling Entertainment. Currently, the former Olympic Gold Medal wrestler is a proud actor and a retired pro wrestler champion.

Kurt Angle has a considerable net worth of about $25 million. Presently he works as a backstage producer for WWE and is making ample revenue out of this business.

Hollywood Hulk Hogan

Hollywood Hulk Hogan is also a retired pro wrestler, but he was THE champion in his time. He is often considered the most renowned pro wrestlers of all time.

Hulk Hogan earned lots of fame and money during his WWF days and reality career that followed. In 2021, Hogan has a sumptuous net worth of about $25 million.

Steve Austin

Steve Austin is famous for having a long and successful career at pro wrestling. He won the world championship in wrestling about six times and the Royal Rumble a record-breaking three times.

His finishing move was the 'Stone Cold Stunner' and fans knew him as 'Stone Cold Steve Austin' during his WWF days, long after he hung up his "Stunning Steve" WCW monikor. When Austin left WWF, he got cast in television shows and movies. Steve Austin has a fancy net worth of about $30 million in 2021.

John Cena

Besides being an American pro wrestler, John Cena is also a popular actor, model, rapper, and bodybuilder. Today he is one of the most popular pro wrestlers. He earned this insane fame after joining WWE.

John Cena is also among the richest pro wrestlers in the world. He currently has a mind-blowing net worth of about $55 million.

Stephanie McMahon

Stephanie McMahon is a former pro wrestler associated with WWE and is famous. She is a corporate executive with an incredible net worth of about $150 million and the daughter of WWE Chairman Vince McMahon. "The Billion Dollar Princess" has played up her daddy's girl gimmick on TV through both heel and hero roles. She is also the spouse of Triple H.

Triple H

Triple H, like his partner Stephanie McMahon, earns a fancy sum from his current corporate vacation. However, he was once a successful pro wrestler with the WWE.

Triple H stands for Hunter Hearst Helmsley, the American Blue Blood gimmick he was given upon his WWE debut in the 1990s. After serving as the WWE executive president and endorsing the WWE brand, Triple H has a fantastic net worth of $150 million when combined with Stephanie McMahon.

The Rock

The Rock or Dwayne Johnson is as famous for his pro wrestling career. He has also been part of the main cast in popular movies like The Mummy Returns and The Fast and The Furious in his acting career. This is one of the reasons why the Rock is the second richest pro wrestler of 2021.

The Rock currently has a jaw-dropping net worth of about $220 million. He is also a social media influencer and a brand endorser.

Vince McMahon

Vince McMahon is reportedly the richest pro wrestler in the world. In 2021, he has a considerable net worth of about $1.6 billion. This insane amount does not come as a surprise as he is the CEO and founder of the most liked entertainment platform, WWE.

His daughter, Stephanie McMahon, who is also on this list of the richest pro wrestlers, is the heiress to WWE. Vince started out as a commentator under his dad's employ in the early days of the WWWF (World Wide Wrestling Federation). As time went on, he took over the business and, in turn, the wrestling world. In the late 90s, he became a pro wrestler himself, while also taking part in film production.
