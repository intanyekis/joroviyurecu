See a Stunning Outtake from Robert Pattinson's Photo Shoot!

Publish date: 2024-08-06

Hubba hubba.

Just nine days away from the anxiously-awaited premiere of The Twilight Saga: New Moon, Vanity Fair has released outtakes from star Robert Pattinson's cover photo shoot by renowned photographer Bruce Weber.

In the shot provided to Usmagazine.com, the 23 year-old actor — a former model, naturally — pops the collar of a natty brown tweed jacket and gives his best brooding sneer.

In the VF interview, Pattinson tells writer Evgenia Peretz that he longs to go back home to London and get a dog — to replace his dearly-departed West Highland terrier, Patty, who died last Christmas. The pooch was "like my sister," Pattinson says.

The reluctant idol also praises his fellow Twilight hunk Taylor Lautner, 17, who's dealing with fame better than Pattinson. Lautner is "much better at doing it [than I am]," Pattinson says. "He's completely handling it. I'm just freaking out all the time."
