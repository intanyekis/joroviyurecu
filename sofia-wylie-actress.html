Wiki/Bio Facts

From Dance to Disney: How Sofia Wylie Became a Star

Sofia Wylie was born on January 7, 2004, in Scottsdale, Arizona, to Chris and Amy Wylie. She has an older sister named Isabella Wylie, who is also a dancer and actress. Sofia Wylie started dancing when she was five years old, and soon developed a passion for it. She trained in various styles, such as ballet, jazz, hip-hop, contemporary, and tap. She also took acting and singing lessons and participated in several competitions and shows.

Sofia Wylie’s talent and charisma caught the attention of many people, including the producers of So You Think You Can Dance and America’s Got Talent, where she appeared as a contestant in 2011 and 2016, and 2015, respectively.

Sofia Wylie’s big break came in 2017 when she auditioned for the role of Buffy Driscoll on the Disney Channel series Andi Mack. She impressed the creators with her acting and dancing skills and landed the part of Andi’s loyal and confident best friend. The show became a hit, and Sofia Wylie gained a lot of fans and recognition for her performance.

Cory, Gina and Mia Brooks: Sofia Wylie’s Iconic Characters

After Andi Mack, Sofia Wylie continued to pursue her acting career and took on various roles in television and film. She starred as Cory Bailey in the 2019 Australian movie Back of the Net. She also joined the cast of High School Musical: The Musical: The Series, a Disney+ original series based on the popular High School Musical franchise. She plays Gina Porter, a transfer student and a talented dancer who competes for the lead role of Gabriella in the school musical. Sofia Wylie’s performance as Gina has been praised by critics and fans alike, and she has also contributed to the show’s soundtrack with songs such as “This is me”, “Everyday”, “Here I Came”, “It’s on” and “Different Way To Dance”. The musical series concluded with the simultaneous release of all eight episodes of season four on August 9, 2023, marking the end of Gina Porter’s character arc. Talking about her time on the show, Sofia expressed her love and gratitude for everyone who worked on the show and shared her experiences of growing up on the show. She also mentioned how the show gave her a taste of high school life, which she never had.

Sofia Wylie has also ventured into the digital space, starring as Mia Brooks, in the YouTube series Shook.

Ironheart and Agatha: Sofia Wylie’s Adventures in Animation and Fantasy

Sofia Wylie is not only a star on the screen but also behind the microphone. She has lent her voice to several animated projects, most notably as Ironheart in the Marvel animated series Marvel’s Spider-Man and Marvel Rising. She also released her debut single “Side by Side” for Marvel Rising: Chasing Ghosts in 2019.

Sofia Wylie also starred as Agatha in the Netflix fantasy film, The School for Good and Evil, based on the book series by Soman Chainani. The movie is directed by Paul Feig.

AIFOS: Sofia Wylie’s Ambitions as a Producer

Sofia Wylie is not only a talented performer, but also a savvy producer. She launched her own production company, AIFOS, in 2019. She also signed with United Talent Agency in 2019.

Sofia Wylie: Model, advocator, and animal lover

Sofia is a multi-talented star who has graced the covers of many famous magazines. Besides her skills in acting and dancing, she has also modeled for Elle Magazine, InStyle, and Teen Vogue. She is a versatile and glamorous celebrity who knows how to shine in different fields.

Sofia Wylie is not only a star on the screen and the stage, but also a champion for mental health awareness. She uses her social media platforms to share her insights and experiences with her fans, especially young girls and boys who struggle with depression, anxiety, and eating disorders. She encourages them to seek help and support and to love themselves unconditionally. She is also a member of the Kate Spade New York Social Impact Council, which is a group of women leaders who work to empower and support women’s mental health and well-being. She attended the global summit on women’s mental health and empowerment in 2023, where she shared her insights and experiences with other inspiring speakers.

Sofia Wylie is also a passionate animal lover. She has a cute puppy named Baby Violent, who is her best friend and companion. She often posts adorable pictures with her pooch on her social media accounts, showing how much she cares for her furry family member.
