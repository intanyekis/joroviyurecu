The Meaning Behind The Song: Try a Little Tenderness by Chris Brown

Publish date: 2024-05-30
TitleTry a Little Tenderness
ArtistChris Brown
Writer/ComposerHarry Woods, Reginald Connelly, James Campbell & Otis Redding
AlbumThis Christmas: Songs from the Motion Picture (2007)
Release DateNovember 20, 2007
GenreR&B, Cover, DMV Rap, Soundtrack, Christmas
DurationNot available
ProducerWyldcard & Bryan-Michael Cox

“Try a Little Tenderness” is a song originally written by Harry Woods, Reginald Connelly, and James Campbell, but it gained popularity through Otis Redding’s rendition. Chris Brown’s cover version was featured on the album “This Christmas: Songs from the Motion Picture” in 2007. The song combines elements of R&B, rap, and Christmas music to create a unique and heartfelt sound.

The lyrics of “Try a Little Tenderness” convey a message of love and compassion towards others, especially those who may be weary or going through difficult times. The song urges listeners to show tenderness and care, rather than judgment or indifference, as a way to support and uplift those around them.

Personally, this song holds a special place in my heart. I remember first hearing it during the Christmas season, and its soothing melody and poignant lyrics resonated with me deeply. I have always found comfort in acts of tenderness and compassion, and this song served as a reminder of the power of love in overcoming hardships.

The opening lines of the song, “Oh, she may be weary, them young girls, they do get weary,” acknowledge the struggles and weariness that individuals, particularly women, may face in their lives. It recognizes the societal pressures and expectations that can weigh them down. The repetition of the phrase “try a little tenderness” serves as a gentle reminder to approach these individuals with empathy and understanding.

The second verse emphasizes the importance of patience and attentiveness in relationships. It urges the listener to anticipate the needs of their loved ones and to make an effort to fulfill them. The line “while she’s there waiting there without them, try, oh, just a little” highlights the desire for emotional support and affection, urging the listener to show tenderness and care.

The chorus of the song reaffirms the central message of offering tenderness and compassion. It urges the listener to show love and affection towards their partner, to never leave them or tease them. Instead, the song encourages holding them softly and squeezing them gently, symbolizing the importance of tenderness in maintaining healthy and fulfilling relationships.

The outro of the song brings the message full circle, with Chris Brown repeating the phrase “just try, just try” as a call to action. He emphasizes the importance of acting with tenderness and encourages the listener not to cheat or mistreat their loved ones.

In conclusion, “Try a Little Tenderness” by Chris Brown is a heartfelt and meaningful song that reminds us of the power of tenderness and compassion in our interactions with others. Its lyrics encourage empathy, love, and support, reminding us to uplift and care for those who may be weary or going through difficult times. Personally, this song has been a source of inspiration and a reminder of the impact we can have on others through acts of kindness and tenderness.
