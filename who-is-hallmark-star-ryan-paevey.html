Who Is Hallmark Star Ryan Paevey?

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Hallmark star Ryan Paevey is a familiar face that has graced both the small and big screen since 2013. The model-turned-actor got his big break on the ABC hit show General Hospital where he played Nathan West for five years. He has since starred in several Hallmark movies, establishing himself as one of the network's leading men.

The 36-year-old star is a man of many talents, he makes handmade jewelry and also doubles as a photographer. He launched his brand, Fortunate Wanderer in 2016. The brand was started as a way for people to acquire Paevey's photography and since expanded. His homemade jewelry, towels, and artwork are available for sale too. Model, actor, artist — Ryan has it all!

He Didn't Set Out To Be An Actor Initially

Ryan started out as a model and even featured in music videos, he worked with some of the biggest names in the industry like Katy Perry and Christina Aguilera. He later went on to land his breakout role, as Nathan west on ABCs General Hospital. He left the soap opera world after five years to pursue other prospects.

Many fans were sad to see him leave the show and speculations about the true nature of his departure from the show arose. While many people assumed that he'd been fired, he set the record straight by revealing that "the decision to leave the show was his."

It's hard to think of Hallmark star Ryan Paevey as anything other than an actor because he was born for it. His stage presence and charisma command attention and anyone that's watched him on screen can attest to this. Surprisingly, he didn't always want to be an actor.

These days, Paevey can be seen on Hallmark, where he stars in several movies on the channel. Most notably, Don't Go Breaking My Heart, Matching Hearts, and Marrying Mr. Darcy. 

He Makes Handmade Jewelry

Ryan loves to take photographs and also makes handmade jewelry, and he's pretty darn good at both. He launched his adventure-inspired brand called Fortunate Wanderer where pieces of his handmade jewelry are available for purchase.

He is a motorcycle enthusiast who takes beautiful photographs on his adventures, Fortunate Wanderer was started as a photography business where he could sell prints of his amazing work. The business was later expanded to include his handmade jewelry and other items.

According to Soaps In Depth, Ryan revealed, "I’d missed using tools and working with my hands, and it made me happy to be doing that again. Now, I work at it daily, and the things I make have become increasingly more complex."
