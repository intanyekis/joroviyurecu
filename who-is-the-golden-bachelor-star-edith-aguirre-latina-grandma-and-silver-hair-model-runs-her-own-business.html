What does 'The Golden Bachelor' contestant Edith Aguirre's do?

Edith is a retired realtor and ex-employee of BROADMOOR Realty, who is quite proud of having started her own firm later in life.

"It's never too late to do whatever you want to do!" she exclaims.

Edith currently has her own clothing and jewelry company, TOZISTAR. It all began with her tailoring for celebrities and a desire to promote her items.

The company's expert workforce is dedicated to meeting the needs of its customers. The brand's garments are one-of-a-kind, and they design bespoke orders. To top it all off, the brand is run and manufactured by women.

Edith is the project manager for the construction of an ADU in her backyard.

She creates lovely hand-painted birthday cards. Edith also works as a silver-haired model.

What is Edith Aguirre looking for in 'The Golden Bachelor'?

Edith is a hopeless romantic searching for someone to share all of life's delights with.

Edith enjoys golfing, salsa dancing, and traveling when she is not working or spending time with her family.

Edith enjoys cooking a large breakfast to eat in her garden on weekends and is searching for a partner to join her.

She despises dating apps, so this exciting opportunity to meet our Golden Bachelor excites her. We can't wait to see how she and Gerry get along.

Who is 'The Golden Bachelor' Gerry Turner?

Gerry Turner is eager to prove that it's never too late to find love. The affable 71-year-old dad from Indiana has been named ABC's first Golden Bachelor, demonstrating that love tales, like so many other things, only get better with age.

When 'The Golden Bachelor' debuts this autumn, Gerry will start handing out roses.

Gerry, who was always a romantic, married his high school love, Toni, in 1974.

With their two children, Angie and Jenny, and subsequently their two granddaughters, Charlee and Payton, they shared a busy and happy life.

Toni died in 2017 following a sudden illness after 43 years together.

Six years after Toni's death, Gerry is ready to put himself out there and discover a love that will endure the test of time in his senior years, with the backing of his family.
